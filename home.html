The Smart Budget Management System is a practical tool for students to effectively manage their finances. Its intuitive interface and key features like transaction logging, warnings, and chart visualization ensure users have real-time insights into their financial health.

Our Features

Allowing users to categorize transactions (income/expenses)

Calculating and displaying real-time balances & providing visual insights through charts.

Issuing warnings when expenses exceed income & offering a reset feature to start fresh whenever needed.
