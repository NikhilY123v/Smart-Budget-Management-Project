Smart Budget Management Systen, is a web-based application designed to help people to track their income and expenses, categorize transactions, and visualize their financial status.

People often struggle to manage their limited financial resources effectively, which can lead to overspending or insufficient savings. This website addresses these issues by providing a simple and efficient tool for tracking finances.

The Smart Budget Management System enables users to log their income and expenses, view categorized summaries, and gain insights into their financial habits through visualizations. This ensures students are informed about their spending patterns and can make better financial decisions.

It features functionalities such as adding income/expenses with date and category, real-time balance updates, warnings when expenses exceed income, a reset feature, and a chart visualization.
